Welcome to the
RTC Exercise

This exercise was developed in collaboration with the AIT, Austrian Institute of Technology and the IAEA, International Atomic Energy Agency, and serves to further educate and sensitize employees with regard to cyber-criminal activities. The scenario constructed here is a fictitious one, but it is based on realistic settings in terms of its basic structure and the behavior of the malware.

Login

In order to participate in the exercises, please log in with your credentials. You will then get access to the documentation and the introduction to the tools to be used, the Exercises-Control and get access to the VNC client from which the exercises can be performed.
